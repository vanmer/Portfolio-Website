Lessoner Website

2021

Client: Lessoner
Services: Web Design, Web Development

The band Lessoner from Leipzig has asked me to design and develop their website. The website serves as a hub for their online presence, linking all their social media appearances and releases. The general concept is based on a grid of cards with each linking to the matching destination. The grid reacts on the user's scrolling through fading in/out of each card. Future projects of the band can be added by linking more cards. Parallax effects for the titles and the custom animated mouse cursor for desktop users gives the website a unique look. Everything is built with a mobil-first approach in mind, giving the user a quick overview of the band on their phone.
