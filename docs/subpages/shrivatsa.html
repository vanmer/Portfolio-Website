Shrivatsa Font

2021

This font is inspired by the ancient symbol found in Tibetan Buddhism, often depicted as a triangular swirl or an endless knot. The typeface is entirely constructed out of rectangle and triangle shapes and is most suited for Display Typefaces purposes.
