The Unseen Landlady

Through unexpected circumstances I ended up living for six weeks in an abandoned apartment. In march 2016, I found myself homeless. Luckily, a friend offered me to stay at the empty apartment that she was about to inherit from her aunt. Due to age and mental illness, her aunt was forced to give up her flat with all her possessions and spent her final years in a retirement home. During my stay there the flat was in a state of transition. My friend was working on renovating the whole place in order to move in herself. Therefore I invited my friend and colleague David to photograph the remaining belongings before they were all gone forever. I have never met this woman and I could only imagine from fragments how she spent her life. This book is a small glimpse into her world.
